Why Tamil-first?

Tamil has one of the longest continuous literary traditions in the world, and one of the least-served in modern NLP. Building for Tamil first — not as a multilingual afterthought — is both a technical bet and a cultural-preservation one.

How I work

Modular architecture, lightweight-first, retrieval quality over raw model size. I adapt open models rather than train from scratch, and I document every project for handoff — to collaborators, and to future me.
